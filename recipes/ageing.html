Agding Potion

Description

This potion goes back in my family for hundreds of years. A small dab will shrink even the largest of warts or most troublesome nose.

Ingredients

  • Eye of newt
  • Toungue of dog
  • Rainwater

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients
  2. Let sit under a blood moon
  3. Boil for three days
  4. Bottle and let cool
